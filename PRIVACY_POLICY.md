# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Early Reader ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information.**

Early Reader operates entirely on your device. All learning progress, user preferences, and app data are stored locally on your device only. We do not:

- Collect personal information (names, email addresses, etc.)
- Track user behavior or analytics
- Share data with third parties
- Use advertising or tracking services
- Transmit any data to external servers

## Local Data Storage

The app stores learning progress and preferences locally on your device using standard device storage mechanisms. This data includes:

- Learning progress and card performance
- User preferences and settings
- Session history

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Children's Privacy

Early Reader is designed for children and their parents. We take children's privacy seriously:

- We do not collect any information from children
- We do not require account creation or registration
- All data remains on the device and is never shared

## Third-Party Services

Early Reader does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to personal information through our app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
